Range Rover SV

The Range Rover SV is the pinnacle of luxury SUV craftsmanship, delivering exceptional off-road capability paired with sumptuous comfort and high-tech features.

Specifications

  • Engine: 5.0L V8 Supercharged
  • Power: 557 hp
  • Torque: 516 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 4.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 140 mph (electronically limited)

Features

  • Luxurious handcrafted interior with customizable options
  • Terrain Response 2 system for optimal off-road performance
  • Meridian sound system for superior audio quality
  • Adaptive cruise control and advanced safety features
  • Panoramic roof and heated/cooled seats

About Range Rover

Range Rover, a marque of Land Rover, has been defining luxury SUVs since 1970. It represents a blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern technology, known for its ability to conquer any terrain in unparalleled comfort.
