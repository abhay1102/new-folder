Mercedes-Benz AMG G63

The Mercedes-Benz AMG G63 is a luxury SUV that combines rugged off-road capabilities with high-performance luxury. Its iconic design and powerful engine make it a favorite among enthusiasts and celebrities alike.

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo
  • Power: 577 hp
  • Torque: 627 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 4.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 149 mph (electronically limited)

Features

  • Distinctive boxy design with premium finishes
  • Off-road capabilities with three locking differentials
  • Advanced MBUX infotainment system
  • Adaptive suspension for enhanced comfort
  • Comprehensive safety and driver assistance systems

About Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes-Benz has been a leader in the luxury automotive market for over a century, synonymous with excellence and innovation in engineering and design.
