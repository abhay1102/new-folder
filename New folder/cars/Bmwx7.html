Car Overview

The BMW X7 is a luxury SUV that combines performance, comfort, and advanced technology. With its commanding presence and spacious interior, it is designed for those who demand both style and practicality.

Specifications

  • Engine:** 4.4L V8 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Power: 523 hp
  • Torque:553 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph:** 4.5 seconds
  • Top Speed:** 155 mph (electronically limited)

Features

  • Spacious 3-row seating
  • Cutting-edge infotainment system with gesture control
  • xDrive all-wheel drive
  • Adaptive Air Suspension
  • Advanced driver assistance systems

About BMW X7

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke, is a German luxury vehicle manufacturer known for its performance-oriented cars and innovative engineering. Since 1916, BMW has embodied the spirit of driving pleasure and luxury.
