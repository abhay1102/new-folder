Car Overview

The Rolls-Royce Phantom is the epitome of luxury and sophistication. With its elegant design and powerful performance, it stands out as one of the most prestigious vehicles on the road.

Specifications

  • Engine: 6.75L V12 Twin-Turbocharged
  • Power: 563 hp
  • Torque: 900 Nm
  • 0-60 mph: 5.1 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

Features

  • Advanced Infotainment System
  • Customizable Interior with Premium Materials
  • All-Wheel Drive
  • Adaptive Air Suspension
  • State-of-the-Art Safety Features

About Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce is a British luxury automobile maker, known for producing high-end motor cars since 1904. The brand symbolizes elegance, prestige, and exceptional craftsmanship.
