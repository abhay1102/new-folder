Mercedes-Benz AMG C63

The Mercedes-Benz AMG C63 is a high-performance version of the C-Class, merging luxury with exhilarating power. Its aggressive styling and sporty dynamics make it a standout in the sports sedan category.

Specifications

  • Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo
  • Power: 469 hp (C63) / 503 hp (C63 S)
  • Torque: 479 lb-ft (C63) / 516 lb-ft (C63 S)
  • 0-60 mph: 3.8 seconds (C63 S)
  • Top Speed: 180 mph (electronically limited)

Features

  • AMG performance exhaust system
  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster
  • AMG Ride Control suspension
  • Active braking assist
  • Luxurious leather interior with AMG styling

About Mercedes-Benz

Founded in 1926, Mercedes-Benz is a German automotive brand known for luxury vehicles, buses, coaches, and trucks. The brand epitomizes safety, quality, and innovation in the automotive industry.
