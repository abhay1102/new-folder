Lamborghini Revuelto

The Lamborghini Revuelto is a hyper-performance supercar that epitomizes innovation, speed, and style. With a powerful hybrid powertrain and an aggressive design, the Revuelto offers a next-level driving experience for enthusiasts who demand extreme performance.

Specifications

  • Engine: 6.5L V12 Naturally Aspirated + 3 Electric Motors
  • Power: 1,001 hp
  • Torque: 725 lb-ft
  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 seconds
  • Top Speed: 217 mph (350 km/h)

Features

  • Hybrid all-wheel drive (AWD)
  • Advanced aerodynamics with active rear spoiler
  • Lamborghini's patented dynamic steering system
  • Advanced digital cockpit with a customizable interface
  • Lightweight carbon fiber construction
  • Adaptive suspension with magnetorheological dampers

About Lamborghini

Automobili Lamborghini is an Italian luxury supercar manufacturer known for creating high-performance sports cars with stunning designs. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini, the brand has become synonymous with speed, luxury, and exclusivity. Lamborghini vehicles are celebrated for their bold styling, exceptional engineering, and thrilling driving experiences.
